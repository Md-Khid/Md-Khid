About Me

A compassionate healthcare professional (a graduate in Bachelor in Health Science-Nursing) who once navigated patient pathways and orchestrated care. But now, his focus has shifted to analysing data structures and information processing. The rhythm of patient heartbeats has given way to the binary hum of code.

The Transition
From the immersive patient follow-ups, he realised that administrative records and the healthcare services received from his patients revealed patterns beyond paperwork and patient experiences. The hidden patterns that held the promise of improving patient outcomes.

The Inaugural Line of Code
Enrolling in a Bachelor Degree in Business Analytics and Masters of Analytics and Visualisation, he traded his healthcare profession to delve deeper into coding and analytics. Loops and logic became his new vitals. With each coding snippet and data analysis, he felt closer to uncovering the hidden patterns that could help improve patient care and outcomes.
