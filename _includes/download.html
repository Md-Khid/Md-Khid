COVID-19 Country Grouping The project utilises basic clustering methods such as K-means, leveraging IBM SPSS Modeller and JMP Statistical Software to categorise countries according to their COVID-19 risk levels. By conducting thorough data exploration and preparation, it examines important factors such as population demographics, GDP metrics and disease prevalence rates. Managing multicollinearity ensures the reliability of the clustering process thereby facilitating the identification of unique risk clusters. These clusters are invaluable for informing policy decisions, optimising resource allocation plans and implementing effective mitigation measures. This thorough analysis provides actionable insights essential for navigating the challenges posed by the ongoing pandemic.
