Credit Card Billable Prediction Findings

This project conducted an in-depth analysis of a credit facility dataset, uncovering insights into customer demographics, credit behaviour and financial trends through various visualisation techniques. It then developed a multiple linear regression model to accurately predict billable amounts, demonstrating its reliability in extrapolating to new data. Through predictive modelling, the project provided actionable insights that aid in resource allocation, risk assessment and strategic planning, thereby enhancing the efficiency and performance of credit facilities.
